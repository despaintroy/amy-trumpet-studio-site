Amy's Trumpet Studio

Private Lessons

My goal is to help everyone find the joy of making music and be enthusiastic about practicing! During lessons, you will improve your skills with:

  • Mouthpiece technique
  • Building a solid routine
  • Rhythm Mastery
  • Music Theory
  • Sight Reading
  • Solo Repertoire
  • Technical Exercises

To schedule a lesson, feel free to contact me by phone 801-644-1283 or email at amydespain365@gmail.com. We meet weekly at a time that works for you. I teach at my apartment or in the music building at Utah State University.

Prices are $20 for a half hour, or $35 for an hour.

Bio

Amy DeSpain began the exciting journey of learning the trumpet at the age of 12 when she quickly fell in love with the instrument. She is currently studying music education at Utah State University with Dr. Max Matzen.

Amy is a regular performer with the American Festival Orchestra, the University's Wind Ensemble, the Caine Brass Quintet, and USU's Symphony Orchestra. In 2018, Amy participated in a trumpet ensemble that performed for the International Trumpet Guild in San Antonio, Texas. In 2022 Amy participated and won USU's concerto competition.

When she is not playing the trumpet, you can find Amy making delicious creations in the kitchen, going for long runs in the sun, or defeating her husband in a board game.
