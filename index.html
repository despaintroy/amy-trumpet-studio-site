Bio

Amy DeSpain began the exciting journey of learning the trumpet at the age of 12 when she quickly fell in love with the instrument. She is now a junior at Utah State University, where she studies with Max Matzen.

Amy is a regular performer with the university's wind ensemble, Caine Brass Quintet, USU’s Symphony Orchestra, and the American Festival Orchestra. In 2018, Amy participated in a trumpet ensemble that performed for the International Trumpet Guild in San Antonio, Texas. In 2022 Amy participated and won USU’s concerto competition.

When she is not playing the trumpet, you can find Amy making delicious creations in the kitchen, going for long runs in the sun, or beating her husband in a board game.

I offer private trumpet and piano lessons for $20 per half-hour lesson.
